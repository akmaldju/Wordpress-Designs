About

Perfect Haircuts

At The Maydy Barbershop, we are dedicated to providing affordably priced, unparalleled hair styling and grooming services that are tailored to every client and their lifestyle in a distinctively unique atmosphere.

The Maydy Barbershop offers both a style and community experience as a true “neighborhood shop.” Our social setting provides our customers with an opportunity to relax and enjoy their surroundings while experiencing a customized level of service. Our barbers and stylists pair modern day cutting edge grooming techniques with timeless traditional services, which proves in fact….

“It Pays to Look Well”.

We recognize your time is valuable, so you are not required to wait at the shop if you have other things to accomplish during the day.  We will call or text you 10-15 minutes prior to your turn so that you can be back to The Shave when your barber or stylist is ready for you.

Services

Pride in Each Service

Haircuts

A standard cut for a shorter, traditional men’s style employing the use of shears or clippers as the stylist sees fit, then cleaning the neck line with clippers.

Shaves

Using pre-shave oils and hot towels to prepare the skin for a close shave using a straight razor outfitted with a protective guard, followed by the application of after shave moisturizer and even more relaxing hot towels. 

Beard Trim

Using clippers or shears to trim or shape facial hair. Finished with a relaxing hot towel.

Mustache Trim

Using clippers or shears to trim or shape facial hair, followed by a razor line up of the jaw, neck and cheeks. Finished with a relaxing hot towel.
